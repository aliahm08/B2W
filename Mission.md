# B2W Mission (Strategy and Focus)

Last updated: 2026-03-03

## Mission Statement

B2W builds AI solutions to improve communications and optimize operational insights.

## Core Offerings and Target Audiences

1. AI services for Individuals.
2. AI consulting for enterprise clients.
3. AI platforms for federal agencies.

## Strategic Directives

- Use a clear four-page structure: `/`, `/individuals`, `/enterprises`, `/government`.
- Keep messaging concise and mission-aligned.
- Avoid fabricated metrics, outcomes, logs, or unsupported claims.
- Keep CTA language consistent across all pages.

## Tracking Note

When product direction changes:

1. Update this mission file first.
2. Reflect UX implications in `Design_Strategy.md`.
3. Record implementation updates in `CHANGELOG.md`.
